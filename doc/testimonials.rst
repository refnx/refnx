Testimonials
------------

Please cite the *refnx* paper if you use it for data analysis in your own publications.
Its full reference is:

    "Nelson, A.R.J. & Prescott, S.W. (2019). J. Appl. Cryst. 52, https://doi.org/10.1107/S1600576718017296."
